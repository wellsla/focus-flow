# **App Name**: FocusFlow

## Core Features:

- Task Organization: Monthly, weekly, and daily organization of tasks, displayed clearly in the UI.
- Application Tracking: Track the progress of job applications.
- Financial Management: Track debts, income, and expenses to get a clear picture of your finances. 
- AI Financial Suggestions: Receive dynamic suggestions based on your financial situation to improve cash flow, powered by AI.
- AI Investment Tips: Get investment tips tailored to your current financial situation using AI as a tool. These will include concrete ways you can invest based on your savings, not just generic investment advice.
- Time Management Analysis: Analyze time spent on social media and games via available APIs (Samsung, Steam, Riot Games) and offer time management suggestions. Will give you feedback with an attitude.
- Performance Dashboards: Interactive dashboards with tables and graphs to track job application performance, remaining benefits, and comparisons with previous months.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#293462) to evoke trust, focus, and sophistication.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#D8E2DC) to create a calm and neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm coral (#EA906A) for CTAs and important alerts, grabbing the user's attention.
- Body text: 'Inter' sans-serif for its clean, readable design, suitable for large amounts of text.
- Headline text: 'Space Grotesk' sans-serif for headlines, lending a computerized look
- Minimalist icons with a focus on clarity and ease of understanding.
- Clean and intuitive layout with a focus on quick access to information and key tasks.
- Subtle animations for transitions and feedback to enhance user experience without being distracting.
